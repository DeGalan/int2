What is this website?

'intrest' was created on October 18, 2021, by myself (Steven DeGalan), as a project for Mathew Kneebone's Interactive 2 class. For this project, I will be interviewing three people: Wendy Barrows, Steven Bruce DeGalan, and myself.

Through these interviews, I hope to encapsulate these individuals' unique personalities, hobbies, and lifestyles. This is as much of a learning experience for me as it may be for you! In a sense, this is a communal project regarding all three of our lives, and is meant to be an educational, entertaining, and interactive journey! I hope you enjoy; thank you for reading this entire wall of text.
